About Me

Hi, I’m Sipo — a relentlessly curious and resourceful aspiring full-stack software engineer based in Zambia. I’m on course to leverage code to solve real-world problems, uplift underserved communities, and have fun doing it.

What Sets Me Apart?

While most developers rely on high-end machines, I’ve built full-stack systems using just my mobile device and Termux, battling inconsistent power and internet access to bring my ideas to life. It’s more than just coding — it’s proof that with enough determination and creativity, any environment can become a launchpad.
